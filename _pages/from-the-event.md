### Results Data

The results data presented here are derived from the **Structured Participatory Discussion session** held during the this project.  
Participants responded to a series of structured choice and ranking questions using Mentimeter, following contextual explanations. All responses were collected anonymously with plots and detailed statistics.

The discussion addressed three main thematic areas:
- **Technical research gaps** related to congestion management  
- **Policy reception and regulatory perspectives**  
- **End users and energy community viewpoints**

The dataset captures stakeholder perceptions and expectations relevant to congestion management and can be used to support research, policy analysis, and scenario development in the Dutch/EU electricity systems.
